About Our Team

In the autumn of 2022, our team collaborated to develop the Husky Scheduler, a platform designed to streamline course planning for the current students at the University of Washington. This website offers a range of features, including course recommendations, course information, prerequisites, and other essential details. Our endeavor aims to provide a user-friendly resource that enhances the course planning experience for the university's student community.
